# Privacy Policy

Personio Attendance Autofill does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

- All processing happens entirely in your browser on the Personio page you are viewing.
- User preferences (work hours, break hours, language) are saved only to `chrome.storage.sync`, which is browser-local and never leaves your device.
- No data is sent to any external server, API, or third party.
- No analytics, tracking, telemetry, or logging of any kind is included.
- The extension only runs on `*.personio.com/attendance/employee/*` pages and has no access to any other websites.

No changes to this policy are anticipated. If any future update requires data collection, you will be notified and asked for consent.
